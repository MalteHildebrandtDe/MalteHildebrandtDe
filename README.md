# Hi, my name is Malte Hildebrandt :)

Atmospheric science student based in Innsbruck, Austria, with a strong interest in data-driven research, interdisciplinary projects, and the practical application of scientific knowledge.

---

## About

I am currently completing a Master's degree in Atmospheric Sciences at the University of Innsbruck in cooperation with the Geosphere Austria (Sonnblick). In parallel, I am enrolled in a Bachelor's program in Educational Science as a complementary field, focusing on science communication, didactics, and knowledge transfer.

My academic and personal work revolves around understanding complex environmental systems, working with data, and making scientific results more transparent and accessible. I am particularly interested in the interface between atmospheric science, operational workflows, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
